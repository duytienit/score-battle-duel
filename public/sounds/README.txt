
Place these sound files in this directory:

1. score.mp3 - A short "pop" or "ding" sound for when a player scores a point
2. time-warning.mp3 - A gentle alert sound for when the timer is about to expire
3. time-up.mp3 - A more prominent alert sound for when the timer expires

For optimal user experience, keep these sounds short (less than 1 second) and not too intrusive.
